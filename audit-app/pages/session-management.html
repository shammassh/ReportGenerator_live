Session Best Practices
Recommended Session Timeouts
  • Access Token: 1 hour (auto-refreshed)
  • Session Cookie: 24 hours (current)
  • Idle Timeout: 2 hours recommended
  • Absolute Timeout: 8-12 hours for workday
Security Guidelines
  • Terminate duplicate sessions for same user
  • Clear expired sessions regularly
  • Monitor stale sessions (no activity)
  • Force re-login after password change
